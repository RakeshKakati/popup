## Response to Common Questions

**Q: Why does it need access to LinkedIn?**
A: To add the "Pop out" button and enable drag-to-save functionality directly on LinkedIn posts.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! All your saved posts are stored locally in your browser. We never upload your data to any server.

**Q: What's the difference between Free and Pro?**
A: Free: Save up to 100 posts. Pro ($9 one-time): Unlimited posts forever + all future updates.

**Q: Does this violate LinkedIn's terms?**
A: No, Popup only helps you organize content you're already viewing. It doesn't scrape or automate LinkedIn.
